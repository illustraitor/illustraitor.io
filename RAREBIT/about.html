About

This is a project I worked on in 2022 for an illustration final. At the time, I was suffering from intense bouts of disassociation and this comic was trying to explore some of those feelings. It was experimental. Some pages were designed to unfold, but it ended up not looking very cohesive in its physical form.

That being said, I'm still fond of this comic. It's one of the few projects I've done so far where I was working through something within me, and I love it when others use art that way.
