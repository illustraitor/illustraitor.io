Welcome to my portfolio site!

My name is Kim Calderwood (they/she) and I graduated CSULB with a degree in Illustration (Pre-production). I enjoy video games, hiking, reading and cheap instant coffee. I have been drawing for most of my adult life, tending to gravitate towards drawing figures and landscapes. Most of my work is digital, mainly because itâ€™s quicker and easier to experiment with. I do think that the versatility of digital mediums does hold a lot of aesthetic charm though, or at least it can. You make make something look organic by way of good quality textures and/or brushes, or you can acheieve sleek and orderly look with vectors. Or you can do a mixed media technique, half on paper/half on the computer. There's so many possibilities! I have played with just about every drawing software I could get my hands on, as well as a good chunk of animating ones. I consider myself to be decently tech literate when it comes to art software, and I intend to keep learning.

I'm always down to make new connections or answer any art-related questions. Feel free to shoot me an email!
