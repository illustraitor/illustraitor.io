About Me

Hello!


Welcome to my portfolio site!



My name is Kim Calderwood (they/she) and I graduated CSULB with a degree in Illustration (Pre-production). I'm in a transitory stage with my work at the moment, still trying to figure out my niche. I'm starting to think my niche is fucking around and finding out. I have made background illustrations, character portraits, small animations, a comic, and a few websites (including the one you are on!). I don't use any ai art programs. While I can see artistic potential in just about anything, the ai stuff just has too much baggage around it to pique my curiosity. Plus I'm all about the process baby, don't take out the best part!

I'm always down to make new connections or answer any art-related questions. Feel free to shoot me an email!
